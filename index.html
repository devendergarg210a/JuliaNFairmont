Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I seal my driveway in Dale City?

For Dale City homes, we typically recommend sealing your asphalt driveway every 2–3 years to maintain optimal protection and appearance.

How long does the sealing process take?

Most residential driveways can be sealed in a single day. The sealant itself needs approximately 24–48 hours to cure completely before you can drive on it.

What's the best time of year to seal a driveway in Dale City?

The ideal time is typically from late spring through early fall, when temperatures are consistently above 50°F and there is no rain forecast for at least 24–48 hours after application.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

Yes, we stand behind our workmanship and offer a warranty on our services. Specific warranty details will be provided during your consultation.
